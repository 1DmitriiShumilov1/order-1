Portuguese Garden

The CD ‘Portuguese Garden’ was recorded February - May 2020, in Portugal, during the days of confinement. It was inspired by nature and its abundance of beautiful plants and flowers. All 15 tracks are of the first and only take of the improvisations without edits in order to convey to the listener, the inspiration and pure reflection of the moment. Each of the songs is an instant improvisation that sprung from the appreciation of the natural world. The track ‘Solitude’, which was the only one to be minimally aligned in terms of composition prior to recording, arose like a meditation and came to bloom just like the early spring cherry blossom in all its glory…

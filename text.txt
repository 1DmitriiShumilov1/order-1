Hello, my name is Vladimir Omeltchenko! I was born in 1964, in Yekaterinburg, Russia, and now I live in Porto, Portugal.
I started showing interest towards music at the age of 5. As for my education, I spent 12 years in the Russian School of Violin, a school for the
best of the best, learning from Lev Mirchin, and graduated from the Ural State Conservatory in Yekaterinburg. During the studies I participated in different competitions, 
winning most of them. I was a Concertmaster of the Chamber Orchestra, Orchestra of Contemporary Music, and  Symphonic Orchestra of the Urals 
Superior National Conservatory. After graduationg from the Conservatory, I became a music teacher and taught in the same Conservatory for several years.

Vladimir Omeltchenko

Hello, my name is Vladimir Omeltchenko! I was born in 1964, in Yekaterinburg, Russia, and now I live in Porto, Portugal. I started showing interest towards music at the age of 5. As for my education, I spent 12 years in the Russian School of Violin, a school for the best of the best, learning from Lev Mirchin, and graduated from the Ural State Conservatory in Yekaterinburg. During the studies I participated in different competitions, winning most of them. I was a Concertmaster of the Chamber Orchestra, Orchestra of Contemporary Music, and Symphonic Orchestra of the Urals Superior National Conservatory. After graduationg from the Conservatory, I became a music teacher and taught in the same Conservatory for several years.

I'm proud of...

  • ...collaborating with the internationally acclaimed composer Giya Kancheli in the writing and arranging of his new music for Violin and Chamber Orchestra, which was composed at the request of Gidon Kremer.
  • ...playing in a Gala Concert sponsored by the Russian Ministry of Culture and directed by American conductor Sarah Caldwell.
